Ishikawajima Harima Heavy Industries 1992

Hub Power Project — Pakistan

Power Generation

The Hub Power Project was one of Pakistan's landmark independent power generation projects and among the largest privately financed power stations in South Asia. Murtaza Corporation supplied industrial piping materials to Ishikawajima Harima Heavy Industries (IHI), earning an official Letter of Appreciation for reliable service and timely deliveries.

  • Year Awarded1992
  • SectorPrivate Sector Power Generation
  • ContractorIshikawajima Harima Heavy Industries (IHI)
  • RecognitionOfficial Letter of Appreciation

Our role: Major supplier of industrial pipes and fittings for the Hub Power Project, supporting construction through dependable material supply and earning formal recognition from the project contractor.

Toyo Engineering 1999

Extension of Pak-American Fertilisers Ltd — Pakistan

Fertiliser Manufacturing

Pak-American Fertilisers Limited (PAFL), Pakistan's first fertiliser manufacturing concern established in 1959, operates a plant at Mianwali producing 1,050 metric tons of urea per day. The 1999 expansion, designed by Toyo Engineering Japan, strengthened one of Pakistan's leading fertiliser production facilities.

  • Production Capacity1,050 metric tons urea/day
  • Year Commissioned1999
  • ClientPak-American Fertilisers Ltd (NFC)
  • Designed ByToyo Engineering, Japan

Our role: Major hardware supplier for the plant extension, establishing a long-term relationship with Pak-American Fertilisers Limited.
