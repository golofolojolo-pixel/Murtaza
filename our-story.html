Our Story

From a small shop to a name the industry trusts.

Twenty-five years, four founders, and one standard that hasn't moved: deliver what you promised, on time, and stand behind it after the sale.

How it started

A small steel shop in Karachi.

Murtaza Corporation began as a small shop dealing in steel — four founders, a handful of clients, and a lot to prove. There was no factory backing it, no big name to lean on. What there was: a willingness to learn the material, the mills, and the standards better than anyone else selling it, and to treat every order as if it were the only one that mattered.

That approach built trust one shipment at a time. Word spread through Karachi's industrial belt that the shop on James Terrace Road did what it said it would — right grade, right certification, right on time. Clients who started with a single pipe order came back for tubes, fittings, flanges, and valves.

How it grew

From one shop to nationwide supply.

As demand grew, so did the operation — a dedicated warehouse in Korangi Industrial Area, certified sourcing relationships with global mills, and a standing commitment to Mill Test Report traceability on every order, no exceptions.

Twenty-five years on, the same four founders who started the shop are still the ones signing off on the work. Projects that once meant a single client in Karachi now stretch across Pakistan — over 500 projects delivered, and counting. The scale has changed. The people answering for the quality have not.

What we believe

Knowledge shared, quality delivered, service that doesn't stop at the sale.

"We beleive in sharing our technical knowledge attained in last 25 years through swiftly delivering our finest quality products and services at reasonable prices, followed by the instant after sales services as customer satisfaction is our foremost priority."

— Murtaza, Founder
